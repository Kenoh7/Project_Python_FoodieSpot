Fried Eggs

  1. Place pan on stove on high heat.
  2. Add some cooking oil on the pan
  3. Crack the egg on a flat surface and open in pan
  4. Add a pinch of salt on the eggs
  5. After 1 minute, Flip the egg to cook on the other side.
  6. After 1 minute, remove egg from pan onto plate.
  7. Add some ketchup and enjoy!
